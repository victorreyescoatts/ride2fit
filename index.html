Why Ride2Fit?

THE PROBLEM

The world of cycling and Running is booming and plenty of Apps are offering different services. However, there is NO App that provides a full range of solutions for cyclists, or a training “community” with riders of a similar level in the same geographical region to coordinate outings. NOR do they provide a customized entry plan to start improving from day one.

THE SOLUTION

Ride2Fit will be the only App in the market in which users will receive an AI interactive training program and an outing suggestions with other cyclists of the same level. Both attributes promote long-term adherence to the App, and help users achieve their riding goals.

This combination of factors makes Ride2Fit's offer unique and innovative compared to the current App market.

Competitive advantage

& USER ENGAGEMENT

  • Ride2Fit does not require many devices, only a bicycle and a cellphone. By keeping it simple, countless cyclists can be reached.
  • The traction of users in Ride2Fit relies on word of mouth, so investment in marketing will optimize awareness over time.
  • Our AI-based developments will ensure friendly feedback on the evolution of physical condition, and updated training programs.
  • Easy matching of outing partners all over the world, will transform Ride2Fit as the best in the global market.

THE PRODUCT

The App will initially profile users with registration information

And suggest an initial training plan. We truly value each rider’s profile, so new programs will be proposed based on the activity of every user.
